I'm a PhD student at the Institute for Logic, Language and Computation (ILLC), University of Amsterdam. My supervisor is Nick Bezhanishvili . My main interests include modal logic, intuitionistic logic, algebraic logic, and duality theory. Recently, I have also developed an interest in arithmetics and categorical logic.
